# Global Sales Operations Intelligence-A SQL and PowerBI Analysis of Northwind Traders

I just completed a Business Intelligence project that challenged me to think like a data analyst for a global gourmet food supplier  and I'm genuinely proud of what came out of it.

THESE ARE THE TOOLS I USED
▸ SQL logic applied during data modelling 7-table relational model with defined relationships
▸ Microsoft Power BI Desktop- 3-page interactive dashboard
▸ Power Query (M Language)- data cleaning, type casting & table transformation
▸ DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) - calculated columns, KPI measures & time intelligence
▸ Excel/CSV- raw data source ingestion

📁 DATASET
The classic Northwind Traders dataset- 7 tables covering Orders, Order Details, Products, Categories, Customers, Employees, and Shippers across fiscal years 1996–1998.

📌 KEY INSIGHTS UNCOVERED
📈 Sales Performance
▸ Revenue peaked in 1997- a 22%+ growth year driven by expanded product listings
▸ Clear Q4 seasonal spikes identified across all years year-end demand is real
▸ A mid-year dip (Jun-Aug) signals a summer seasonality pattern worth addressing

🛒 Product Intelligence
▸ Beverages leads all categories at ~31% of total net revenue
▸ A single SKU; Côté de Blaye, contributes a disproportionate share of category revenue
▸ Discontinued products account for ~8% of historic revenue- a forward risk if not replaced

🌍 Regional Insights
▸ USA, Germany & Austria are the top 3 markets- together they dominate the revenue share
▸ Austria & Ireland carry disproportionately high freight costs relative to their order volumes
▸ Cities like Cunewalde, Graz & Boise emerged as top revenue-generating urban centres

🚚 Operational Efficiency
▸ Speedy Express = fastest delivery avg; United Package = best cost-speed balance
▸ Federal Shipping has the highest freight cost- renegotiation could save $8K-$12K/year
▸ On-Time Delivery rate sits at ~89%, with Q4 being the most strained period
▸ Margaret Peacock leads employee sales; clear performance spread across the team

🧠 WHAT I BUILT
✅ Page 1: Sales & Revenue Overview- KPI cards, trend lines, geo map, quarterly bars
✅ Page 2: Product & Category Intelligence- donut, funnel, scatter chart, top-10 SKUs
✅ Page 3: Regional, Operational & People- shipper benchmarks, freight map, employee rankings
✅ Calculated columns: GrossRevenue, NetRevenue, DaysToShip, ShippingStatus, ProductStatus
✅ Full DAX Measures table with 15+ KPIs
✅ Calendar table for time-intelligence functions
✅ 5 interactive slicers: Year | Country | Category | Shipper | Employee

This project pushed me to go beyond building charts; it required asking the right business questions, engineering the data model correctly, and translating raw numbers into executive-ready insights.
